I am a 19 year old freelance artist currently attending Wilfrid Laurier University, sited in Brantford, Ontario. I aspires to acquire a profession within User Experience Design in the future. I have had the passion of creation throughout my entire life and would love to turn this passion into a career I can love. I have experience working with a variety of clients and creating products and designs such as advertisements, social media work, abstract artwork, prototypes, photography and much more.
